Resources

Step #1

We firstly recommend checking out Google Maps to see what modes of transportation are available in your area.

Google Maps

Download Google Maps to plan your commute with walking, biking, or public transit directions.

Step #2

After step #1 you should decide what mode of transportation will be most convenient for you, also taking consideration into external variables (for example traffic).

Step #3

Next check out one of the following website resources to explore and learn. If using public transit be aware of schedule constraints and delays.

Step #4

Test out that mode of transportation to see if you feel comfortable and safe using it.

Caltrain

Plan your trip using Caltrain, a convenient rail service connecting the Bay Area.

SamTrans

Find bus routes and schedules for SamTrans, serving San Mateo County and beyond.

BART

Use BART for fast and reliable transit across the San Francisco Bay Area.

Bay Area Bicycle Coalition

Explore cycling resources, maps, and advocacy efforts in the Bay Area.

Design Tech Commute Page

Visit the official Design Tech High School page for commuting and transit information.
